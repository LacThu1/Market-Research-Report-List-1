<p><strong>The Household Non-invasive Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CPAP</li><li>BiPAP</li><li>APAP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household non-invasive ventilator market includes three main types: CPAP, BiPAP, and APAP. CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) delivers constant airflow to keep airways open, primarily used for obstructive sleep apnea. BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) offers two pressure settings for inhalation and exhalation, accommodating patients with more complex respiratory needs. APAP (Automatic Positive Airway Pressure) adjusts pressure automatically throughout the night based on detected breathing changes, providing personalized therapy. Each type caters to varying patient requirements.</p></p>
