Edge lighting utilizes LEDs positioned along the borders of a panel, providing uniform illumination. Bottom lighting involves placing light sources at the base of the display, creating an even light distribution. Each type serves unique applications, catering to diverse needs in display technology and enhancing visual experiences in electronic devices.</p></p>
