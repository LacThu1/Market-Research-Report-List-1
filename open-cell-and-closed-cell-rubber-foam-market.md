<p><p>The rubber foam market is categorized into open cell and closed cell types, each utilizing various materials. Open cell foams, known for their air permeability, typically use NR (Natural Rubber) and EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), providing flexibility and insulation. Closed cell foams, which prevent air and moisture penetration, include EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate), CR (Chloroprene Rubber), PVC/NBR (Polyvinyl Chloride/Nitrile Rubber), SBR (Styrene-Butadiene Rubber), and Neoprene, offering durability and resistance in diverse applications. Others encompass specialized formulations for niche markets.</p></p>
