<p><strong>The ASIC Design Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Cell Based ASIC</li><li>Gate Array Based ASIC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ASIC design service market segments into Standard Cell Based ASICs and Gate Array Based ASICs. Standard Cell Based ASICs utilize pre-designed logic cells, allowing for customization and faster time-to-market, ideal for complex applications with high integration demands. In contrast, Gate Array Based ASICs offer a predefined array of gates that can be configured, providing more flexibility but typically at a longer development timeline. Both types cater to varying customer needs in cost, performance, and design complexity.</p></p>
