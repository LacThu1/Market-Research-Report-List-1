<p><p>The body fat measurement market encompasses various technologies, each offering unique advantages. Bioimpedance analyzers (BIA) utilize electrical currents to estimate body composition. Skinfold calipers assess fat thickness at specific body sites for simple evaluations. Hydrostatic weighing (HwD) measures body density through water displacement, providing accurate fat measurements. Air Displacement Plethysmography (ADP) utilizes air displacement to determine body volume and composition. Dual Emission X-ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) employs X-rays to analyze bone and soft tissue, delivering precise body fat assessments.</p></p>
