<p><p>The semiconductor strain gauge sensors market comprises two key types: naked gauges and backed gauges. Naked gauges feature a thin, adhesive film that directly measures strain on the surface, offering high sensitivity and precision. In contrast, backed gauges have a protective backing, enhancing durability and ease of installation, making them ideal for rough environments. Both types are widely used in various applications, including structural health monitoring and industrial machinery, capitalizing on their ability to detect minute changes in strain.</p></p>
