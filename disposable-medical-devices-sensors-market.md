<p><p>The disposable medical device sensors market features various types catering to diverse healthcare needs. Ingestible sensors are designed to monitor internal processes after being swallowed. Implantable sensors are embedded within the body for long-term health monitoring. Strip sensors are often used for quick, surface-level diagnostics, such as glucose testing. Invasive sensors require penetration of the skin to measure physiological parameters, providing direct and often real-time data for clinical decision-making. Each type enhances patient care through its unique application.</p></p>
