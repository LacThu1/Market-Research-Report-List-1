The gene therapy market includes retroviral vectors (RV), which integrate into the host genome but are limited to dividing cells; adenoviral vectors (AdV), which can deliver genes without integrating, allowing transient expression; and adeno-associated viruses (AAV), known for their safety and ability to integrate minimally, providing effective long-term gene expression across various cell types.</p></p>
