<p><p>The arc welding torch market includes two primary types: air-cooled and water-cooled welding torches. Air-cooled torches rely on ambient air to dissipate heat, making them lightweight and suitable for less intensive applications. In contrast, water-cooled torches utilize circulating water to manage heat, allowing for high-performance welding tasks in heavy-duty environments. Both types cater to different user needs, with air-cooled options favored for portability and ease, while water-cooled versions are preferred for prolonged, high-energy welding operations.</p></p>
