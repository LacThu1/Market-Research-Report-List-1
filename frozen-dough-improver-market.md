<p><ul><li>Universal Type</li><li>Special Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frozen dough improver market is categorized into two main types: universal type and special type. Universal type improvers are versatile and can be used across various dough formulations, enhancing texture and shelf life without being specific to any particular product. In contrast, special type improvers are tailored for specific applications, such as pizza or pastry dough, providing targeted benefits like improved elasticity and fermentation control. Both types aim to optimize dough performance and quality during freezing and baking processes.</p></p>
