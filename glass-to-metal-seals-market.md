<p><ul><li>Matched Seals</li><li>Compression Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass-to-metal seals market includes two primary types: matched seals and compression seals. Matched seals are designed with glass and metal components that share similar thermal expansion properties, ensuring a secure bond under temperature variations. This type is ideal for applications requiring high stability and minimal stress. Conversely, compression seals utilize a glass rod or tube that compresses against the metal during installation, allowing for flexibility in sealing and accommodating movement. Both types are crucial for ensuring durability and reliability in various industries.</p></p>
