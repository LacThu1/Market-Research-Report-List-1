<p><p>The cigarette lighters market is categorized into three main types: liquid lighters, gaseous lighters, and gas-liquid mixing lighters. Liquid lighters, often using lighter fluid, provide a steady flame and are refillable. Gaseous lighters utilize pressurized gas, such as butane, offering portability and convenience. Gas-liquid mixing lighters combine both gas and liquid to create an adjustable flame, appealing for precise fuel delivery. Each type caters to different user preferences in functionality, ease of use, and design.</p></p>
