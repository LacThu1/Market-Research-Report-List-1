<p><ul><li>Flexo</li><li>Gravure</li><li>Offset Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The doctor blade market is crucial in the flexo, gravure, and offset printing sectors, where it is used to control ink transfer and achieve optimal print quality. In flexographic printing, doctor blades remove excess ink from the plate, ensuring precise application. In gravure printing, they maintain ink leveling on engraved cylinders, delivering consistent results. In offset printing, doctor blades facilitate ink distribution on rollers, enhancing performance. The demand for efficiency and high-quality output drives advancements in doctor blade technology across these applications.</p></p>
