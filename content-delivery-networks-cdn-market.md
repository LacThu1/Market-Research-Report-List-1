<p><p>Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) are distributed networks of servers that deliver web content and other assets to users based on their geographic location. By caching content closer to users, CDNs enhance the speed, reliability, and performance of web applications, reducing latency and improving user experience. The growth of the CDN market is significantly driven by increasing internet usage, the rise of streaming services, and the demand for faster content delivery.</p><p>The Content Delivery Networks (CDN) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period. Key trends shaping this market include the adoption of edge computing, which allows processing to occur closer to the data source, and the growing emphasis on cybersecurity solutions integrated within CDN services to protect against DDoS attacks and other threats. Furthermore, the surge in online gaming and the proliferation of Internet of Things (IoT) devices are increasing reliance on CDNs. As businesses continue to prioritize digital transformation, investments in CDN services are expected to rise, enabling them to deliver superior content experiences to end-users across various platforms and devices.</p></p>
