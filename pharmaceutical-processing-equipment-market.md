<p><p>The pharmaceutical processing equipment market comprises various types, each serving specific functions. Agitators mix substances for uniformity, while blowers facilitate efficient material movement. Capsule equipment is essential for filling and sealing capsules, ensuring accurate dosages. Centrifuges separate components based on density, vital for purification processes. Dryers remove moisture from products, enhancing stability, while granulators convert powders into granules for easier handling and processing. Other equipment includes diverse tools tailored to meet specialized needs in pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
