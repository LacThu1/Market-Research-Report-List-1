<p><p>The wrap-around cartoning machine market is categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic. In contrast, automatic machines operate independently, streamlining the entire cartoning process from product feeding to sealing, enhancing efficiency and speed, and catering to high-volume production needs. Both types meet varying operational requirements in packaging industries.</p></p>
