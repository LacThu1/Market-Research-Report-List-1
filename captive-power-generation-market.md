<p><p>Captive power generation refers to electricity generated primarily for self-consumption. </p><p>1. **Cogeneration** involves producing both electricity and useful heat simultaneously from the same energy source, enhancing efficiency.</p><p>2. **Tri-Generation** expands on this by adding cooling to the mix, typically through absorption chillers, addressing heating and cooling needs.</p><p>3. **Quad-Generation** further integrates additional outputs like chilled water, providing comprehensive energy solutions.</p><p>4. The **normal market** focuses on standalone power generation, catering to local demand without integrating heating or cooling outputs.</p></p>
