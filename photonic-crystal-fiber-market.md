<p><strong>The Photonic-crystal Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Index-Guiding Fibers</li><li>Photonic Bandgap (Air Guiding) Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photonic-crystal fiber market is primarily categorized into two types: index-guiding fibers and photonic bandgap (air guiding) fibers. Index-guiding fibers utilize variations in the refractive index to confine light within the core, enhancing efficiency and allowing for various applications in telecommunications and sensing. In contrast, photonic bandgap fibers leverage structured air holes running along the length to create a photonic bandgap, trapping light in the core and enabling unique properties such as low-loss transmission and the ability to guide multiple wavelengths effectively.</p></p>
