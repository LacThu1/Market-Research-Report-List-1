<p><p>The metallic microspheres market is segmented into hollow and solid microspheres. They enhance insulation and reduce weight in various products. Solid microspheres, on the other hand, are denser and are often used in construction materials, coatings, and electronics, providing structural integrity and improving thermal stability.
