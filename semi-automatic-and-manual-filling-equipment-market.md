<p><p>The semi-automatic and manual filling equipment market includes two key types of filling systems. Manual filling equipment, on the other hand, relies completely on the operator for filling, offering greater flexibility but often resulting in slower production rates. Both types cater to varying production scales, with semi-automatic machines suitable for medium to high-volume needs and manual systems ideal for smaller or niche applications.</p></p>
