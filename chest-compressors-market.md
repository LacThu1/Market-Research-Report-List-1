<p><p>The chest compressors market can be divided into two primary types: mechanical and electric. Mechanical chest compressors use manual leverage systems to deliver compressions during cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR), often requiring direct operator involvement. In contrast, electric chest compressors are powered by motors, providing automated and consistent compressions with minimal human intervention. Both types serve critical roles in emergency medical services, enhancing patient outcomes by ensuring timely and effective chest compressions during cardiac arrest situations.</p></p>
