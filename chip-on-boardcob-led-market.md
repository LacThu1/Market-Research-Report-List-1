<p><p>The Chip-on-Board (CoB) LED market features various types, including lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip designs. Lateral chips are positioned parallel to the substrate, enabling compact layouts and efficient heat dissipation. Vertical chips, stacked for high density, excel in power applications due to their thermal performance. Flip chips have an inverted configuration that enhances electrical interconnection and reduces package size, making them suitable for high-performance lighting solutions. Each type offers unique advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
