Raschel machines are known for their versatility and capability to produce complex, open structures, making them ideal for applications like lace and mesh fabrics. In contrast, Tricot machines are recognized for their high-speed operation and ability to create smooth, flat, and tightly woven textiles suitable for lingerie, sportswear, and technical fabrics. Both types serve distinct segments within the textile and apparel industry.</p></p>
