<p><p>The polymer stabilizer market encompasses three primary types: antioxidants, heat stabilizers, and light stabilizers. Antioxidants prevent oxidative degradation during processing and service, prolonging the lifespan of polymers. Heat stabilizers protect materials from thermal degradation by maintaining integrity under high temperatures. Light stabilizers absorb harmful UV radiation, minimizing photodegradation and discoloration. Together, these stabilizers enhance the durability, performance, and aesthetics of polymer products, making them essential across various applications in industries like automotive, packaging, and consumer goods.</p></p>
