<p><p>The cell counting spectrophotometer market features two primary types: single-mode readers and multi-mode readers. Single-mode readers are specialized devices designed for specific applications, primarily focusing on measuring cell concentration or viability using one detection method. In contrast, multi-mode readers offer versatile functionality, supporting multiple detection methods such as absorbance, fluorescence, luminescence, and others, making them suitable for a wider range of assays. This flexibility makes multi-mode readers particularly popular in research and clinical laboratories.</p></p>
