<p><p>The medical suction regulators market comprises continuous and intermittent suction regulators. Continuous suction regulators maintain a steady vacuum pressure for consistent fluid removal, ideal for procedures requiring uninterrupted suction, such as surgeries or intensive care. In contrast, intermittent suction regulators provide suction in brief bursts, allowing for controlled fluid drainage during specific phases of treatment, often used in wound care or respiratory therapy. Each type serves unique clinical needs, enhancing patient safety and procedural efficiency.</p></p>
