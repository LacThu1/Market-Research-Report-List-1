<p><ul><li>Real-Time PCR</li><li>Traditional PCR</li><li>Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymerase chain reaction (PCR) market is segmented into three main types: Real-Time PCR, Traditional PCR, and Digital PCR. Real-Time PCR enables the simultaneous amplification and quantification of DNA, offering rapid results for diagnostics and research. Traditional PCR focuses on amplifying specific DNA sequences in a simpler, more cost-effective manner, though without real-time analysis. Digital PCR provides ultra-sensitive quantification by partitioning samples into individual reactions, enhancing accuracy for low-abundance targets. Each type serves distinct applications in molecular biology and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
