<p><strong>The Endotoxin Detection Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Clot Endotoxin Test</li><li>Chromogenic Endotoxin Test</li><li>Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endotoxin Detection Service Market offers various testing methods to identify endotoxins, primarily Gel Clot, Chromogenic, and Turbidimetric Tests. The Gel Clot Endotoxin Test employs the use of limulus amebocyte lysate (LAL) to form a gel in the presence of endotoxins, signaling contamination. The Chromogenic Endotoxin Test uses colorimetric changes to quantify endotoxins, providing more sensitive results. The Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test measures the turbidity caused by endotoxin-laden LAL reactions, enabling rapid detection and quantification in pharmaceutical and biomedical applications.</p></p>
