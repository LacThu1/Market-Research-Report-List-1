<p><strong>The Jet Dispensing Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Jet Valves</li><li>Non-contact Jet Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Jet Dispensing Valve market is segmented into two main types: contact and non-contact jet valves. Contact jet valves operate by directly touching the substrate, allowing for precise material deposition, which is ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. In contrast, non-contact jet valves dispense materials without physical contact, using air pressure to create droplets, making them suitable for delicate surfaces or high-speed production lines. Both types cater to diverse industries, including electronics, automotive, and medical manufacturing.</p></p>
