<p><p>The Vision and Navigation System for Autonomous Vehicles comprises various market types, including cameras, sensors, positioning systems, and inertial measurement units (IMUs). Cameras facilitate real-time environmental perception and object detection, while sensors gather data on vehicle surroundings through radar, LiDAR, or ultrasonic technologies. Positioning systems, utilizing GPS and other technologies, ensure accurate location tracking, and IMUs measure vehicle acceleration and rotational rates, contributing to stability and motion awareness. Together, they enhance decision-making and navigation accuracy in autonomous systems.</p></p>
