<p><p>The shoulder arthroplasty products market includes various types: Hemiarthroplasty, which replaces one side of the shoulder joint; Total Shoulder Arthroplasty, offering complete joint replacement for those with severe arthritis; Reverse Total Shoulder Arthroplasty, designed to address rotator cuff issues by reversing the normal ball-and-socket configuration; and Resurfacing Shoulder Arthroplasty, which preserves more bone by capping the joint surfaces instead of fully replacing them. Each type targets specific shoulder conditions to improve functionality and reduce pain.</p></p>
