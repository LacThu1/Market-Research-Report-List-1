<p><p>The wooden frame fire windows market includes two main types: fixed fire windows and movable fire windows. Fixed fire windows are permanently installed, providing a robust barrier against fire and heat, making them suitable for high-risk areas such as stairwells or fire egress paths. Movable fire windows, on the other hand, can be opened or closed as needed, allowing for ventilation while maintaining fire safety when shut. Both types are essential for ensuring building safety and compliance with fire regulations.</p></p>
