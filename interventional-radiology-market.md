CT scanners deliver cross-sectional images, crucial for precise targeting and assessment during procedures. Angiography systems focus on vascular imaging, allowing clinicians to visualize blood vessels and diagnose conditions effectively. Each type contributes significantly to improving patient outcomes in interventional radiology.</p></p>
