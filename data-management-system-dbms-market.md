<p><p>A Data Management System (DBMS) is software that enables the creation, manipulation, and management of databases. The market is primarily divided into two types: Relational and Non-Relational (NoSQL). </p><p>Relational DBMS organize data into tables with predefined schemas, facilitating complex queries using SQL. Non-Relational DBMS, on the other hand, offer flexible data models like key-value pairs, documents, or graphs, accommodating unstructured or semi-structured data. Each type serves specific use cases, catering to differing scalability, performance, and data complexity needs.</p></p>
